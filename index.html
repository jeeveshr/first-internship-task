Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury & Hospitality

Since 2011, The Grand Horizon has been the epitome of luxury hospitality. Our hotel blends timeless architecture with modern comforts, creating a sanctuary where every guest feels like royalty.

From our Michelin-starred restaurants to our award-winning spa, every detail has been meticulously crafted to provide an unforgettable experience. Our dedicated team of hospitality professionals ensures your stay exceeds every expectation.
